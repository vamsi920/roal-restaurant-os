# Contact page role (`/contact`)

**Prompt 57** — keep `/contact`; do not compete with `/demo`.

## Split

| Route | Role | Primary action |
|-------|------|----------------|
| `/demo` | Self-serve **sample** — video slot, call flow, transcript, KDS ticket | Book demo call (mailto) or sign up |
| `/contact` | **Human outreach** — pilot interest, menu/rush context, fit check | Mailto / pilot form |

## Rules

1. **Contact owns** pilot form, `hello@getroal.com`, rush/menu fields, “good fit” checklist, self-serve signup escape hatch.
2. **Demo owns** illustrative call content — no duplicate transcript/KDS walkthrough on contact.
3. **Contact CTAs** lead with email or form; `/demo` is a secondary “see sample first” link, not a co-equal hero CTA.
4. **Nav** — “Book a demo” may point to `/contact` (live walkthrough); footer “Demo” points to `/demo` (static sample).
